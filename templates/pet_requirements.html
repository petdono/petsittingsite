Pet Requirements (Dogs, Cats, Small Animals, Reptiles)

All animals:

  • Must follow the guide below.
  • Provide proof of any treatments, full names and descriptions of pets, and any relevant medical history.
  • ID must be provided before you leave.
  • Must be spayed/neutered (if applicable). Animals may not be in heat or pregnant.
  • Show locations of food, toys, cages, crates, medicines, or other supplies.
  • Agree to the Terms & Conditions
  • Animals not listed below may be denied or have special requirements.

If it barks:

  • Must be up-to-date on vaccinations, check-ups, and must have a recent flea treatment.
  • Provide leash, collar, and any medications
  • Must have a secure, fenced-in area for bathroom breaks (if applicable) or allow walks on a leash.
  • Provide food and water bowls, food, and treats
  • Disclose any behavioral issues

If it meows:

  • Clean litter box and provide fresh litter prior to visit.
  • Must be up-to-date on vaccinations, check-ups, and must have a recent flea treatment.
  • Provide litter, food, and any medications
  • Disclose any special needs

If it squeaks, chirps, hops, or scurries:

  • Provide cage, bedding, and food
  • Cages for small animals should be escape-proof, chew-proof, and cleaned prior to visit.
  • Cages must be the appropriate size for the animal, matching information available online.
  • Disclose any health concerns.

If it has scales:

  • Provide enclosure, heating, and food.
  • Ensure the enclosure is escape-proof and secure.
  • Clean the enclosure before the visit.
  • Disclose any special care instructions.
